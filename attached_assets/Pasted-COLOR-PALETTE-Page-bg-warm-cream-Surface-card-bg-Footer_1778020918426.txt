COLOR PALETTE
Page bg (warm cream)
Surface / card bg
Footer bg (olive-dark)
Primary text (dark charcoal)
Secondary text (warm gray)
Brand accent (terracotta-red)
Divider / border
Card & drawer white
- Page background: warm cream #F5F2EC — never pure white
- Product card background: slightly cooler cream #EDEAE2
- Brand accent color: muted terracotta-red #B75C47 — used ONLY for:
    primary CTA buttons, "Add to Cart", checkout button,
    hover underlines on brand names, progress bar in cart
- Footer background: deep olive-green #4A5240 — high contrast against cream body
- All borders: 1px solid warm gray #D4C9B8 — soft, never harsh
- No pure black anywhere. Darkest text is charcoal #2C2C28
- Zero blues, purples, or bright colors — palette is entirely earth-toned
TYPOGRAPHY
LOGO:
- Wordmark only: "ELEVARE" in wide-tracked uppercase
- Font: a clean geometric sans (similar to Futura or Jost)
- A small dot sits below the "E" — minimalist brand mark
- Color: dark charcoal #2C2C28 on light bg; cream on dark bg (footer)

HEADINGS / DISPLAY TEXT:
- Font: an editorial serif — closely resembles "Cormorant Garamond" or "Freight Display"
- Used for: section titles, hero headline, footer tagline
- Key behavior: mixed serif + sans within same headline
  Example: "The Spring [Renewal]" — "The Spring" in sans, "Renewal" in italic serif
  Example: "The [Elevare] Standard" — "Elevare" switches to italic serif inline
  Example: footer tagline "[Thrive] With Us" — "Thrive" in italic serif script weight
- This mixed-weight, mixed-style headline treatment is the #1 signature of this design
- Heading sizes: hero ~48–56px, section titles ~32–38px, product page FAQ ~36px
- Font weight for serif: 400 italic only — never bold serif

BODY TEXT:
- Font: a humanist sans-serif — similar to "DM Sans" or "Neue Haas Grotesk"
- Weight: 300 (light) for paragraphs, 400 for UI labels, 500 for prices and CTAs
- Size: 14px body, 12px captions, 11px meta text
- Line-height: 1.7 for paragraphs, 1.4 for compact UI

NAVIGATION LINKS:
- Font: same sans, weight 400
- Size: 14px
- Case: Title Case (not uppercase, not all-caps)
- Color: charcoal #2C2C28, no underline
- Dropdown arrows: small chevron SVG inline

BRAND NAME LABELS (above product title on cards):
- ALL CAPS, letter-spacing: 0.12em, font-size: 10–11px
- Color: terracotta accent #B75C47
- This is a critical detail — brand appears above product name in accent color

PRICES:

MICRO-INTERACTIONS & MOTION
- Cart drawer: slides in from right with ease-out timing (~250ms)
- Accordion expand: smooth max-height transition (~200ms)
- Marquee: continuous CSS scroll, no pause, seamless loop
- Scroll reveal: sections fade in + slight translateY(20px) as they enter viewport
- Button hover: slight opacity reduction (0.85) on terracotta buttons
- Product image hover: very subtle scale(1.02) on image only, not card
- No jarring transitions, no bounce, no spring physics
- Wishlist heart: fills with terracotta on click (toggle state)
- Progress bar in cart: smooth width transition when items added
KEY DESIGN PRINCIPLES TO REPLICATE
1. MIXED TYPOGRAPHY HEADLINES — the single most recognizable feature.
   Every major section title mixes italic serif with upright sans in the same line.
   This is not optional — it defines the brand voice visually.

2. TERRACOTTA AS THE ONLY ACCENT — one color does all the work:
   CTAs, brand labels, progress bars, icon accents, active states.
   Everything else is neutral earth tones.

3. CREAM BACKGROUND, NOT WHITE — the warm #F5F2EC base makes
   every element feel editorial and premium, not clinical.

4. BRAND NAME ABOVE PRODUCT NAME — in ALL CAPS terracotta.
   This is Elevare's card signature. Replicate exactly for BlessingSells.

5. OLIVE FOOTER = ANNOUNCEMENT BAR — same color bookends the page.
   Creates a visual frame — the page feels contained and intentional.

6. FLAT CARDS, NO SHADOW — product cards have zero elevation.
   They live on the page, not above it.

7. NEARLY-SQUARE BUTTONS — border-radius: 3–4px only.
   Not pill-shaped, not fully square. Just barely rounded.

8. CART IS A DRAWER, NOT A PAGE — slides from right, never navigates away.
